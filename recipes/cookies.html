Odin's Cookies


Photo by Talha Uğuz from Pexels

The ultimate, super simple Odin recipe for unforgettable chocolate chip cookies.

Description

Whether you're with your loved ones or by yourself, Odin's Cookies
will 100% make you smile right after the first bite.

Yet another Odin Recipes hit, we're sure. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugar, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown

Et voila, Odin's Cookies are served!
