Odin's Peanut Bars


Photo by Polina Tankilevitch from Pexels

These peanut butter bars are tasty like no other. Odin-guaranteed!

Description

Forget store-bought peanut butter candy. Odin's Peanut Bars are next level.
Perfect for all of your sudden, sweet cravings.

Brought to you by Odin Recipes. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix together cracker crumbs, sugar, butter or margarine,
    and 1 cup peanut butter in a bowl until well-blended
  3. Press evenly into the bottom of a 9x13-inch pan
  4. Place chocolate chips and 4 tablespoons peanut butter in a microwave-safe
    bowl, then microwave on high, stirring every 15 seconds until smooth
  5. Spread mixture over crust
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour then cut into 12 squares

Et voila, Odin's Peanut Bars are served!
